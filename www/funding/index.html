Donations

Developing DDNet and running servers costs money and is a lot of work. Donations of any amount are appreciated. Don't forget to tell me the name you want to be listed with in the list of donors:

From some countries you can send money to friends with PayPal without any fees. For this you have to send it manually to deen@ddnet.tw, as shown in this German example image. If you want to donate by another method (bank transfer, money cards, ...) or you're interested in sponsoring a whole server, get in touch with deen.
