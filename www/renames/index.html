Renames

Rules regarding in-game player renames are as follows:

  • The original name should have 3k or more points on it
  • Your last rename should be at least one year ago
  • You must be able to provide proof of owning the points being moved
  • The names shouldn't be banned
  • No exception will be made whatsoever

If you request a rename and then later change your mind, know that it won't be reverted until at least one year has passed. Think carefully.

If you would like a rename, contact an admin on Discord.
