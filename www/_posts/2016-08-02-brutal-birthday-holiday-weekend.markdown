[<img class="demo" src="/_uploads/birthday3.png" />](//forum.ddnet.org/viewtopic.php?f=33&p=42062)

- Saturday, August 6, 20:00 CEST: New Brutal (2\*) map "*Tropical Island*" by RayB. The first team to finish wins.
