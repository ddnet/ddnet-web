Getting in Touch

You can find us on the DDraceNetwork servers, our DDNet Discord server and our Teamspeak server on ts.ddnet.tw. Otherwise you can post in our Forum for suggestions, questions or anything else. There's also an IRC channel #ddnet on Quakenet (Webchat, Logs).

Join the Team

We are always looking for new people to join our team. If you want to support us, you can send in your applications in the Staff Recruitment Forum section.
