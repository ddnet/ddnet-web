Staff

DDraceNetwork was an idea by Megaman with support by Delo and onionly .me' in the summer of 2013 and was initially implemented by deen. For new features or bugs talk about them in the Forum or suggest them on Github. New maps can be submitted on Discord, where our Testers will take care of them. Please note the Mapper Rules.

Getting in Touch

You can find us on the DDraceNetwork servers, our Discord server and our Teamspeak server on ts.ddnet.tw. Otherwise you can post in our Forum for suggestions, questions or anything else. There's also an IRC channel #ddnet on Quakenet (Webchat, Logs).

Join the Team

We are always looking for new people to join our team. If you want to support us, you can send in your applications in the Staff Recruitment Forum section.
